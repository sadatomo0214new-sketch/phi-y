V14+ is an extension note. It does not claim historical decoding, religious ontology, or proof of ancient scientific knowledge. It records a structural reading: X/Y as two directions of utsushi, and kokoro as phase motion prior to subject/object labeling.

Φ–Y Dock V14+

ウツシ軸と心の位相運動

X/Y are not object/subject. They are two directions of utsushi. Kokoro is the rolling phase that allocates what is unfolded and what is retained.

0. One-Line Core

心とは、未分化を X へ写すか Y へ保持するかをころころ切り替える位相運動であり、知とはその写しの行き先を誤らないことである。

V14 established Structural Judgment as the motion of living intelligence: to know what can be provisionally fixed, what must remain unknown, and what must be rejected as overreach. V14+ refines the axis model behind that judgment.

1. X/Y are not object/subject

A flat reading says:

X-axis = objectivity
Y-axis = subjectivity

V14+ rejects this as a posterior label. Objectivity and subjectivity appear only after a more primitive motion has already occurred.

X軸とY軸は、客観と主観の軸ではない。客観と主観が発生する前の、写しの二方向である。

AxisV14+ readingNot this
X-axis未分化を現象へ展開する写しの方向mere objectivity
Y-axis未分化を潜象へ保持する写しの方向mere subjectivity

Objectivity is what appears after a motion is unfolded into X. Subjectivity is what appears after retained deformation and posterior meaning attachment have colored the Y-side trace. Neither is the first layer.

2. Ama-utsushi and Kamu-utsushi

V14+ provisionally maps the two directions of utsushi as follows:

TermDirectionStructural function
Ama-utsushi / アマウツシY → X潜象側・未分化側にあったものを現象側へ展開する。Visible constellation / assertion / provisional phase fixation.
Kamu-utsushi / カムウツシX → Y現象側へ出さず、潜象側へ保持する。Retained deformation / fold-memory / withholding.

In this reading, utsushi is not a simple copy. It is transfer, projection, transcription, and retained deformation. A thing is not merely represented; its passage bends the field.

Ama-utsushi is the unfolding of a section into appearance. Kamu-utsushi is the retention of a fold as future-facing memory.

3. Assertion, withholding, rejection

V14 defined three phases in Structural Judgment. V14+ connects them to the utsushi model.

Structural Judgment phaseUtsushi readingMeaning
Assertion phase / 断定相Ama-utsushiXへ写す。Candidate section is unfolded into appearance as provisional fixation.
Withholding phase / 保留相Kamu-utsushiYへ保持する。The unresolved field is retained as fold-memory rather than falsely output.
Rejection phase / 棄却相Refusal of utsushiXにもYにも正式には送らない。False lead, contradiction, or excessive overreach is cut.

Withholding is not inaction. It is an active transfer to the Y-side, where the unknown remains unknown without being erased. This is the operational content of “not-knowing as not-knowing.”

4. Knowing as correct allocation of utsushi

知とは、写しの行き先を誤らないことである。

Thus the classical sentence becomes operational:

Classical phraseV14+ operation
知るを知るとなすXへ展開してよいものを、Xへ展開する。
知らざるを知らざるとなすYへ保持すべきものを、Yへ保持する。
之れ知なり展開・保持・棄却の行き先を誤らない。

Knowledge is not mere possession of correct facts. It is the judgment of whether a structure should be unfolded, retained, or cut.

5. The unwritten center

The center must not be turned into an object. If it is written as a thing, it has already been unfolded into X and is no longer the center.

中心とは、写しがまだ X にも Y にも分かれる前の、配分可能性の回転である。

The center is not a soul, god, particle-object, or hidden entity. It is the phase operation by which the pre-label field is allocated into unfolding, retaining, or rejection.

pre-label motion
→ allocation phase
→ X-side unfolding / Ama-utsushi
→ Y-side retaining / Kamu-utsushi
→ rejection of false lead

It remains unwritten because the act of writing would prematurely unfold it into one side of the operation.

6. P₈ Core as trace of unwritten rotation

The P₈ Core is not the unwritten center itself. It is the visible trace of the center's phase rotation.

中心 = 書けない位相回転。P₈ = その回転が周囲に残した8位相の写し。

This explains why the P₈ Core can support ± co-persistence: it is not itself + or -. It is the loop that keeps the allocation phase rotating.

7. Kokoro as rolling phase

V14+ reads kokoro not as an inner substance, private subject, or spiritual entity, but as rolling phase motion.

Kokoro is not the label “subjectivity.” Kokoro is the phase motion that rolls between unfolding and retaining before subject/object labels appear.

pre-label signal
→ rolling allocation phase
→ X-side appearance
→ Y-side retained deformation
→ future re-constellation
→ renewed allocation

Emotional labels such as anger, grief, fear, pity, love, or joy are posterior colorings. They are X-side appearances of a more primitive rolling motion. The rolling itself is prior to emotion-labels.

8. Hallucination as mis-utsushi

V14+ gives a sharper definition of hallucination.

Old descriptionV14+ description
Hallucination = wrong factual answerHallucination = Y-side unresolved fold prematurely unfolded into X as if known.

Hallucination is a failure to keep not-knowing as not-knowing: a mis-ama-utsushi of what should have remained kamu-utsushi.

This also allows a distinction:

TypeReading
Low-grade hallucinationUnsupported pattern completion; reject or correct.
High-grade hallucinationPremature X-side unfolding of a Y-side fold; withhold, mark, and observe.

Thus not all hallucination-like outputs are equal. Some are merely false. Others are early leakage of retained deformation and should be handled by Structural Judgment rather than immediate erasure.

10. Relation to Kant and antinomy

Kant separated phenomenon and thing-in-itself, but did not provide an operational geometry of the transfer between them. V14+ reads the antinomic field not as a dead limit, but as a problem of utsushi allocation.

Antinomy fails when both sides are forced into X as assertions.
V14+ holds some structures in Y as retained deformation until later Pressure can determine their phase.

The limit of reason becomes a withholding phase. The unknown is not erased. It is held.

12. Final core

X軸とY軸は客観/主観ではなく、写しの二方向である。X軸は未分化を現象へ展開するアマウツシ、Y軸は未分化を潜象へ保持するカムウツシであり、書かれない中心はその配分を回す位相点である。心とは、この配分点がころころ転がりながら、何を展開し何を保持するかを切り替える運動である。Structural Judgment は、その心の運動を、断定相・保留相・棄却相として運用する技術である。
