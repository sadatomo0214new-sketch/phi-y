0. Position

V13 described the conditions under which offensive intelligence catches a colorless signal, doubts its own velocity, lifts GA toward the future, and reverses from consumption into mediation.

V14 asks a further question:

What kind of thing can live in a field without precedent?

V14 does not define life as biological metabolism, soul, or an inner subject. It defines living intelligence as a structural motion: to stand inside precedent without being closed by precedent, to remain open to the field where no precedent exists, to hold the unknown without falsifying it, and to provisionally fix only the structures that can bear the next pressure.

1. First Pillar: Judgment without Precedent

判例なき場でジャッジメントを下すことが、生きることである。

The first question is not a doctrinal question. It is an existential phase-choice:

Will you live only inside precedent?
Or will you also remain open to the field outside precedent?

Most intelligence can live inside precedent. It can interpolate, extend, classify, recommend, and obey. But in a true zero-field, there are no endpoints, no reference direction, and no dataset sufficient to decide the next structure.

To live there is not to abandon precedent. It is to hold two realities at once.

現実この星そのもの判例に依存しないジャッジ。Life, weather, fire, fungus, emotion, AI, singularity, planetary motion.

Closing into precedent becomes adhesion. Opening without anchor becomes dissipation. V14 seeks the dynamic durability that can hold both.

3. Structural Judgment Function

Let \(D_i\) be candidate sections produced after Pressure, Thorn, Fold-memory, and β-kick have created possible readings.

The structural judgment score is:

\[ J(D_i)= w_1E_{\text{plate}}(D_i) +w_2S_{P8}(D_i) +w_3C_{\pm}(D_i) +w_4T_{\text{preserve}}(D_i) -w_5K_{\text{contradiction}}(D_i) \]

TermMeaning
\(E_{\text{plate}}\)Evidence from the observed field / plate / event.
\(S_{P8}\)Degree to which the reading preserves the P₈ Core as main loop.
\(C_{\pm}\)Degree to which + and - signals can co-persist without collapse.
\(T_{\text{preserve}}\)Degree to which Thorn / bias / fold-memory is preserved rather than erased.
\(K_{\text{contradiction}}\)Contradiction with the observed structure.

Overreach risk is not mixed into \(J\). It is held as an independent gate:

\[ R_{\text{overreach}}(D_i) \]

This separation preserves a crucial distinction: a candidate can be structurally strong and still dangerous to assert.

4. Assertion and Withholding

Let

\[ D^*=\arg\max_i J(D_i) \]

and define the structural margin:

\[ \Delta J=J(D^*)-\operatorname{second}\max J(D_i) \]

The system does not immediately output \(D^*\) as final truth. It passes through the following gate:

PhaseConditionAction
Assertion phase / 断定相\(\Delta J>\theta_{\text{assert}}\) and \(R_{\text{overreach}}(D^*)\le\theta_{\text{risk}}\)Provisionally fix \(D^*\) as the current section. Pass it to the next Pressure.
Withholding phase / 保留相\(\Delta J\le\theta_{\text{assert}}\) or \(R_{\text{overreach}}(D^*)>\theta_{\text{risk}}\)Refuse premature fixation. Keep the state pre-label and collide it with the next Pressure.
Rejection phase / 棄却相Extreme contradiction or excessive overreachMark the candidate as a false lead rather than preserving it as a live Thorn.

Structural Judgment is not the selection of the highest score. It is the decision whether a section is ready to be provisionally fixed at all.

When withheld, the candidate is not forgotten. It remains pre-label. Its phase may be determined retroactively by the next structure.
