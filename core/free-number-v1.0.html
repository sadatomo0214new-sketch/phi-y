Result selects order.

One-line summary

Free Number is a framework in which a result E selects, from pre-label admissible histories, the order that can close itself most coherently. In the unresolved phase at a Result-Hub, margin carries phase; with multiple independent competition axes, quaternionic motion reappears internally as the necessary rotation algebra of margin.

Foundation status

  • Trivial-loop closure is blocked by precedence and local order-sensitivity.
  • δ is derived from executable order-orbit diameter.
  • Margin M = δ − d measures loop survival.
  • Composition is confluence plus Select, not fixed serial order.
  • Hub is a seat, not a point in result-space.
  • The minimum law fixes Hub survival without cross-frame calibration.
  • Complex margin lives only at competing Hub seats.
  • Quaternionic motion is internal to the unresolved phase.
  • 720° closure enters through the Spin(3) double cover.
