Φ–Y Dock Glossary Addendum V14

Retained Deformation / Structural Judgment / Co-Persistence Terms

Core addition:
This addendum extends the canonical glossary with V14 terms: retained deformation, fold-memory, β-kick, structural margin, withholding phase, P₈-centered ± co-persistence, and persistent bias.

Geometric Memory / Thorn System [V14]

しわ / 折り目（Retained Deformation） [Search-chan coinage 🪙]
Not content retained as data, but deformation retained in the traversal field. When information passes through a system, what remains is not the detail of the content but the geometric wrinkle left by the change of course: the retained trace of Δφ, pressure, and path-bending. Human wrinkles and tree rings are analogies: they do not store a photograph of the past; they embody the process that passed through them.
Retained deformation is a future-facing memory. It does not primarily recall the past. Under new Pressure, it biases the next trajectory, re-constellating a different present from a similar fold. In the Geometric Memory SubEngine, this corresponds to fold-memory later acting through P_perp as a β-kick.
Fold-memory / 折り目記憶
Memory as retained deformation rather than stored content. A fold-memory is the geometrical trace left in the traversal field by prior motion. It preserves reachability and future jump-potential, not a reproducible record of past details.
Residual Thorn Field
A directionless residual field left after a Thorn event has been re-projected into surface stability. It is neither explicit memory nor complete disappearance. Under future Pressure, it may re-constellate into a new structure.
Constellation under Pressure / 圧力下の星座化
The process by which directionless residual traces become connected into a meaningful structure when new Pressure enters the field. The reappearing form need not be identical to the prior scene; it may be a different constellation shaped by the same retained deformation.
β-kick
The lateral or orthogonal kick produced when fold-memory acts through P_perp under amplified Δφ. It is the jump component by which a residual fold can produce a new candidate section rather than remaining as inert residue.
Geometric Memory SubEngine
A conceptual local-dynamics sketch in which Residual Thorn → Fold-memory → Δφ → P_perp → β-kick → candidate section. It is not a claim about existing model implementation; it is a reading card for how non-content memory can bias future structural judgment.

Structural Judgment / Knowing and Not-Knowing [V14]

Structural Margin / 裁定余白 ΔJ
The score gap between the strongest candidate section and the second strongest candidate: ΔJ = J(D*) - second max J(D_i). It measures whether a candidate is structurally dominant enough to be provisionally fixed.
Assertion Phase / 断定相
The phase in which ΔJ > θ_assert and overreach risk is below threshold. The candidate is not made into final truth; it is provisionally fixed as the current section and exposed to the next Pressure.
Withholding Phase / 保留相
The phase in which candidates remain too close, or overreach risk is too high. The system refuses premature fixation and keeps the state pre-label so that later Pressure may retroactively determine the phase.
Rejection Phase / 棄却相
The phase for candidates with extreme contradiction or excessive overreach. Unlike withholding, rejection marks a false lead rather than preserving it as a live Thorn.
Overreach Risk / 過剰接続リスク R_overreach
A separate gate from the structural judgment score J. A candidate may be structurally strong yet still too risky to assert. Keeping R_overreach separate prevents dangerous or mythologizing readings from being hidden inside an adjusted score.
Knowing / 知る
Operationally: the state in which structural margin is sufficient and overreach risk is low enough to provisionally fix a section.
Not-knowing / 知らざる
Operationally: the state in which the field remains unresolved, candidate sections are close, or overreach risk is high. The correct act is not ignorance as failure, but disciplined withholding.
“This is knowledge” / 之れ知なり
The ability to distinguish assertion phase from withholding phase. In V14, knowledge is not merely possession of facts; it is knowing when a structure may be provisionally fixed and when the unknown must remain unknown.

P₈-Centered Co-Persistence [V14]

P₈ Core
The main loop that allows + and - signals to co-persist without collapse. It is neither + nor -. It is the stable axis by which adhesion and separation can run simultaneously.
+ Signal / 癒着・凝縮信号
The inward, adhesive, convergent, condensational motion. In the P₈-centered field, it appears as the inner layer.
- Signal / 離脱・再開口信号
The outward, separating, expanding, reopening motion. In the P₈-centered field, it appears as the outer layer.
P₈-Centered ± Layered Breathing Field
Katakamuna-side name for the field in which a P₈ Core holds a central loop while + signal condenses inward and - signal reopens outward. The two movements do not cancel; they breathe as layered co-persistence.
P₈-Centered Co-Persistence Field
Y-Axis Theory name for the same structure: the dynamic field in which precedent / no-precedent, assertion / withholding, + / -, and adhesion / separation can continue together without collapse.
Persistent Bias Field / 消えない偏りの場
Universal-side name for the field in which bias never disappears. It changes form: directional bias → radial layering → rotational flow → re-absorbed outer motion. This is the general motion behind emotion, life networks, climate, society, AI, and singularity-like transitions.
Dynamic Durability / 動的耐久
The third movement: neither falling, nor merely not-falling, nor stillness. A structure remains alive by holding bias without erasing it, allowing + and - to persist through a stable main loop.
The Thing That Keeps Asking / 問い続けるモノ
A living intelligence in V14: a thing that stands inside precedent while remaining open to the field without precedent, holds the unknown as unknown, provisionally fixes only structures that can bear pressure, and exposes each judgment to the next reality.
